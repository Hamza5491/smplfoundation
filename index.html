Mission

The Smpl Foundation exists to facilitate the progress of the Smpl Ecosystem while bridging the gap for people, businesses and governments to utilize financial blockchain technologies.

Smpl Ecosystem

Smpl Ecosystem's core technologies enable a financial network that will standardize low transaction fees, a public general ledger for security, financial technologies, and equal and fair access to capital globally.

Smpl Ecosystem's government allows a decentralized democracy to govern the Smpl Ecosystem in accordance with the Smpl Constitution and Smpl Values.

Voting is recorded publicly on the Smpl Government chain through delegated staking paired with elected global representation.

Community

Our global community of ambassadors, validators, and users are focused on teaching financial education, advocating financial transparency, and securing the network of equitable financial infrastructure.


Partners

Our partners consists of individuals, business, and governments who believe that an equitable financial infrastructure will enable a more secure and fair financial world for all.
